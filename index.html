Postmortem

Looking back on our development process, there are several areas we would have expanded given more time.

Core Systems & Infrastructure

  • Level Progression System: A more robust progression framework with multiple levels, unlockable content, and meaningful difficulty scaling.
  • Audio System: Background music, sound effects for player actions, and audio feedback to enhance experience and provide clearer gameplay cues.
  • Save/Load System: Proper serialization and deserialization to allow players to save their progress and resume gameplay sessions.
  • Keybind Customization: A settings menu enabling players to remap controls to their preferences.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Monster-Plant Interactions: Enemies that actively attack and damage player crops.
  • Seed Packet System: Limited-quantity seed packets that spawn randomly throughout levels, or alternatively, a full shop system for purchasing seeds and supplies.
  • Tool Inventory: An inventory management system for tools.
