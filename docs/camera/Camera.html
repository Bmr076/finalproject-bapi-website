    Struct Camera

    struct Camera;

    Constructors

    NameDescription
    this (w_, h_) Initialize camera rectangle. Typically w = SCREEN_WIDTH and h = SCREEN_HEIGHT.

    Fields

    NameTypeDescription
    mCameraRect sdl.rect.SDL_FRectRepresents the camera's view into world space. It is w units wide, h units high and starts centered at (0,0) in world space. For now, we assume world scale and camera scale are the same (no zoom).

    Methods

    NameDescription
    CameraPointToScreen (camPoint) Translate a point from camera space to screen space.
    CameraRectToScreen (camRect) Translate a CenteredRect from camera space to an SDL_FRect in screen space which can easily be rendered.
    MoveCamera (deltaX, deltaY) Move the center of the camera to (x+deltaX, y+deltaY) while keeping it in bounds of the world space.
